**Social Media Do’s and Don’ts**

---

|Do|Don'ts|
|-|-|
|1. **Keep your account passwords strong and secure**|1.**Don’t reuse the same password for all accounts**|
|2.**Verify information before sharing**|2.**Don’t believe or forward unverified news**|
|3.**Be mindful of your digital footprint**|3. **Don’t post anything that can harm your future career**|
|4.**Connect with professionals and learning communities**|4.**Don’t accept requests from unknown or suspicious accounts**|
|5.**Report inappropriate or harmful content**|5. **Don’t ignore cyberbullying or online harassment**|
